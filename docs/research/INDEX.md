|------|---------|-------------|
| `dji-gimbal-software-control/` | Research on all software pathways for controlling DJI gimbals | Covers official SDKs (RS SDK, MSDK, OSDK, PSDK), open-source projects, CAN/DUML/BLE protocols, commercial PTZ solutions; `report.md` is the synthesis, `background.md` has raw sources |

| `microcontroller-selection/` | Comparing MCU/SBC options for camera slider with gimbal control | ESP32-S3 recommended; covers ESP32 variants, Pico 2W, Teensy 4.1, STM32, BeagleBone; evaluated for WiFi, CAN bus, stepper control, power; `report.md` is the synthesis, `background.md` has raw sources |
| `slider-motor-selection/` | Stepper motor and drive system selection for the slider axis | Recommends NEMA 17 17HS19-2004S1 (59 Ncm, 2.0A) + GT2 belt + 20T pulley + TMC2209 at 24V; includes torque calculations for 7 kg payload at various inclines; precision analysis for focus stacking (belt ~50-100 um real-world); speed analysis (0.5-1.0 m/s fast reposition); noise assessment with StealthChop; `report.md` is the synthesis, `background.md` has raw sources |
| `long-travel-linear-motion/` | Research on linear motion systems for 900-1500mm travel | Belt vs ball screw vs lead screw vs rack-and-pinion; MGN12 rails on aluminum extrusion; critical speed calculations; closed-loop steppers; DIY CNC community approaches (PrintNC, LowRider, OpenBuilds); recommends GT2 belt + closed-loop stepper + optional linear encoder; `report.md` is the synthesis, `background.md` has raw sources |
| `linear-guide-system/` | Research on linear guide rail and extrusion options for the slider frame | Compares MGN12 vs MGN15 rails, V-slot wheels vs linear rails, aluminum extrusion profiles (2040, 4040), mounting methods; 7 kg payload, 900-1200mm travel; `background.md` has raw sources (no synthesis report yet) |
| `vertical-drive-motor-sizing/` | Motor sizing, brakes, and self-locking for vertical slider operation | Recommends GT2 belt + NEMA 17 with 70 Ncm power-off electromagnetic brake (17HS19-2004D-B070, $29); full torque calcs for 3.5 kg real / 7 kg design load; self-locking analysis (T8x2 lead screw too slow, worm gear too slow, belt+brake is optimal); optional constant-force spring counterbalance; commercial slider vertical derating data (iFOOTAGE: 50% derating); thermal analysis; `report.md` is the synthesis, `background.md` has raw sources |
